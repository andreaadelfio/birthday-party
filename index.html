FAQ

Can I bring a plus-one?

If your invitation includes one, yes. If not, just ask first so the planning stays accurate.

Is there a dress code?

Nothing formal. Just come tidy and comfortable.

What about food and drinks?

There will be food, drinks, and non-alcoholic options. Use the RSVP note field for allergies or preferences.

Should I bring a gift?

Presence matters more than presents. If you want to bring something, keep it thoughtful and simple.
